# Clinician Positioning And Trust Blueprint

## Positioning
Fleming is the clinician-facing medical AI built for workflow completion, not just evidence lookup.

Core claims:
- `Workflow-complete`: structured outputs for clinical summary, drug safety, stewardship, and med review
- `Trust-visible`: evidence count, confidence, guideline signal, and caveats are surfaced in-product
- `Measurable`: release quality is benchmark-gated with tracked thresholds
- `Clinically elegant`: desktop and mobile experiences are fast, calm, and scannable

## Anti-OpenEvidence Narrative
Use this framing externally and internally:
- OpenEvidence helps clinicians look things up.
- Fleming helps clinicians finish the work with visible trust and benchmark-backed quality.

## Trust Principles
- No strong recommendation without evidence context.
- No hidden uncertainty.
- No release without a benchmark snapshot.
- No core workflow without a benchmark-backed output format.
- No trust messaging without a measurable artifact behind it.

## Core Clinician Workflows
- `Clinical Summary`
  - one-liner
  - active problems
  - plan
  - watch items
- `Drug Interactions`
  - ranked severity
  - mechanism
  - monitoring
  - alternatives
- `Stewardship`
  - syndrome framing
  - empiric plan
  - de-escalation triggers
  - duration
- `Med Review`
  - duplication and interaction risk
  - deprescribing opportunities
  - highest-risk medications
  - follow-up monitoring
