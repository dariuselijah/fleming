# Design Partner Program

## Goal
Recruit 20-30 clinicians to pressure-test Fleming on real de-identified workflow problems and turn those learnings into product, trust, and benchmark improvements.

## Ideal Partner Mix
- primary care
- hospital medicine
- urgent care
- residents
- pharmacist-adjacent medication safety champions

## Operating Cadence
- Weekly 30-minute workflow review sessions
- Partners bring one de-identified case or one repeated workflow pain point
- Fleming team captures:
  - time-to-useful-output
  - what had to be manually corrected
  - missing-data prompts that should exist
  - trust signals that felt useful or insufficient
  - opportunities to add benchmark cases

## Session Template
1. Define the job to be done.
2. Run the clinician’s current workflow.
3. Run the same job in Fleming.
4. Compare speed, trust, and output usability.
5. Convert failures into backlog items and benchmark cases.

## What To Collect
- specialty
- care setting
- workflow category
- case complexity
- answer usefulness score
- trust clarity score
- output edit burden
- whether the answer would have been used in real practice

## Success Definition
- repeated weekly engagement
- at least one strong workflow proof point per specialty cluster
- benchmark additions every week from partner feedback
- case-study candidates by day 60

## Outreach Copy
Fleming is building the most trustworthy and measurable clinician AI. We are looking for clinicians who want to shape structured workflows for summary, medication safety, stewardship, and med review using de-identified cases. Design partners get early access, a direct product line, and a say in what gets benchmarked before release.
